Who We Are

Welcome to Bhartiya Stree Shakti :Women Relief Trust, is going to be establish soon in 2021. We are probably living in the worst time our modern society has ever seen in terms of women security. We aim to make women feel as strong as ever and strong enough to fight the parasites of our society, strong enough to fight the odds, strong enough to protect themselves against any sexual assaults. We aim at giving power to those without whom we cease to exist. Among the worst countries in crime, India has an abhorrent track record in all forms of sexual exploitation. In homes, on streets, in public transports and even offices. We are focusing on building an effective, fast and reliant system to make the women of India feel safe and empowered.This tool will act as eyes and ears for the police and help them in preventing crimes against women. Apart from this we also use to ask from other capable people to help them with their time and money.for more details you can visit our whole website.
